Before we begin, you should know…

Filing your Pennsylvania Personal Income Tax return (PA-40) in myPATH is free.

myPATH can handle these return types:

  • Wages and salaries reported on up to 64 federal Forms W-2
  • Miscellaneous income reported on up to 64 federal Forms 1099-MISC
  • Interest income reported on up to 2 Pennsylvania Schedules A
  • Dividend income reported on up to 2 Pennsylvania Schedules B
  • Profit or loss from a business or profession reported on up to 20 Pennsylvania Schedules C
  • Sale, exchange, or disposition of property reported on up to 2 Pennsylvania Schedules D
  • Rents and royalty income reported on Pennsylvania Schedule E
  • Other deductions claimed on Pennsylvania Schedule O
  • Refund donations to Pennsylvania 529 College Savings Program Accounts reported on Pennsylvania Schedule P
  • Tax forgiveness credit claimed on Pennsylvania Schedule SP
  • Gambling and lottery winnings reported on Pennsylvania Schedule T
  • Allowable unreimbursed employee business expenses reported on the PA Schedule UE
  • Health insurance coverage information questions reported on the REV-1882
  • Other dependent and dependent enhancement tax credit claimed on the REV-1882

You must file a paper return or use another type of electronic filing method if:

  • You have income, expenses, or credits that myPATH does not permit
  • You are a first time filer and are not a resident of Pennsylvania (with a Pennsylvania Driver License/Photo ID)

If you owe $1 or less, you do not have to submit a payment with your return.

If applicable, have these potentially required documents nearby and available to be electronically uploaded with your return:

  • Form(s) W-2
  • Form(s) 1099R
  • Form(s) 1099-MISC
  • Form(s) 1099-NEC
  • Form(s) 2441
  • Form(s) 1040 Schedule 3
  • Birth certificate(s) and Social Security card(s) for dependents claimed for tax forgiveness purposes
  • Other supporting documentation (paystubs, receipts, statements, etc.)
